## Lead Engineer: Syllabus Outline

**I. Foundations & Core Skills**

*   **A. Leadership Principles:**
    *   1. Defining Leadership: Styles, Qualities, and Impact.
    *   2. Situational Leadership: Adapting to Team Needs.
    *   3. Leading with Influence vs. Authority.
    *   4. Ethical Leadership and Decision Making.

*   **B. Communication & Collaboration:**
    *   1. Effective Verbal and Written Communication.
    *   2. Active Listening and Feedback Techniques.
    *   3. Conflict Resolution and Negotiation Skills.
    *   4. Facilitating Meetings and Workshops.

*   **C. Technical Proficiency (Deep Dive):**
    *   1. Domain Expertise: Mastery of relevant technologies.
    *   2. Architectural Patterns and Design Principles.
    *   3. Code Review Best Practices and Standards.
    *   4. Performance Optimization and Scalability.

**II. Team Management & Development**

*   **A. Team Formation and Dynamics:**
    *   1. Building High-Performing Teams.
    *   2. Understanding Team Roles and Responsibilities.
    *   3. Fostering a Collaborative and Inclusive Environment.
    *   4. Managing Remote and Distributed Teams.

*   **B. Mentoring and Coaching:**
    *   1. Identifying and Developing Talent.
    *   2. Providing Constructive Feedback and Guidance.
    *   3. Setting Clear Expectations and Goals.
    *   4. Creating Development Plans.

*   **C. Performance Management:**
    *   1. Performance Reviews and Appraisals.
    *   2. Identifying and Addressing Performance Issues.
    *   3. Promoting Growth and Recognition.
    *   4. Compensation and Promotion Strategies.

**III. Project Management & Delivery**

*   **A. Project Planning and Execution:**
    *   1. Agile Methodologies (Scrum, Kanban).
    *   2. Requirements Gathering and Analysis.
    *   3. Estimating and Prioritization.
    *   4. Risk Management and Mitigation.

*   **B. Stakeholder Management:**
    *   1. Identifying and Managing Stakeholder Expectations.
    *   2. Communicating Project Progress and Challenges.
    *   3. Building Strong Relationships with Key Stakeholders.
    *   4. Negotiation and Conflict Resolution with Stakeholders.

*   **C. Delivery and Continuous Improvement:**
    *   1. Ensuring Quality and Reliability.
    *   2. Monitoring and Tracking Project Progress.
    *   3. Conducting Retrospectives and Identifying Improvements.
    *   4. Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery (CI/CD).

**IV. Strategic Thinking & Vision**

*   **A. Technical Strategy and Roadmap:**
    *   1. Aligning Technical Strategy with Business Goals.
    *   2. Creating and Communicating a Technical Roadmap.
    *   3. Evaluating and Selecting Technologies.
    *   4. Identifying and Addressing Technical Debt.

*   **B. Innovation and Research:**
    *   1. Encouraging Innovation and Experimentation.
    *   2. Staying Up-to-Date with Emerging Technologies.
    *   3. Evaluating and Implementing New Technologies.
    *   4. Contributing to the Open Source Community.

*   **C. Organizational Leadership:**
    *   1. Influencing Organizational Decisions.
    *   2. Advocating for Engineering Best Practices.
    *   3. Driving Technical Culture and Innovation.
    *   4. Contributing to the Overall Success of the Organization.

**V. Advanced Topics & Specializations (Optional)**

*   **A. Specific Technology Deep Dives:** (e.g., Cloud Computing, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity)
*   **B. Advanced Leadership Techniques:** (e.g., Servant Leadership, Transformational Leadership)
*   **C. Enterprise Architecture:** (e.g., TOGAF, Zachman Framework)
*   **D. Scaling Engineering Organizations:** (e.g., Processes, Structures, and Culture)
