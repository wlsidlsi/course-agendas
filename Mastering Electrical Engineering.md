# Electrical Engineering: Foundations & Applications

**I. Circuit Analysis Fundamentals**

*   **Week 1:** Introduction to Electrical Engineering, Basic Circuit Concepts (Voltage, Current, Power, Energy), Circuit Elements (Resistors, Capacitors, Inductors), Ohm's Law
*   **Week 2:** Kirchhoff's Laws (KCL, KVL), Series and Parallel Resistor Combinations, Voltage and Current Dividers, Power Dissipation in Resistors
*   **Week 3:** Nodal Analysis, Mesh Analysis, Source Transformations, Linearity and Superposition
*   **Week 4:** Thevenin's Theorem, Norton's Theorem, Maximum Power Transfer Theorem

**II. Time-Domain Circuit Analysis**

*   **Week 5:** First-Order Circuits (RC and RL Circuits), Natural Response, Step Response, Time Constant
*   **Week 6:** Second-Order Circuits (RLC Circuits), Overdamped, Critically Damped, and Underdamped Responses, Natural Frequency and Damping Ratio
*   **Week 7:** Transient Response Analysis Techniques, Initial and Final Value Theorems

**III. AC Circuit Analysis**

*   **Week 8:** Sinusoidal Steady-State Analysis, Phasors, Impedance and Admittance
*   **Week 9:** AC Circuit Power Analysis (Instantaneous Power, Average Power, Reactive Power, Apparent Power), Power Factor, Power Factor Correction
*   **Week 10:** AC Circuit Analysis Techniques (Nodal, Mesh, Superposition, Thevenin/Norton) for AC Circuits
*   **Week 11:** Frequency Response, Bode Plots, Resonance

**IV. Semiconductor Devices & Circuits**

*   **Week 12:** Introduction to Semiconductor Materials, Diodes (Ideal and Practical Models), Diode Circuits (Rectifiers, Clippers, Clampers)
*   **Week 13:** Bipolar Junction Transistors (BJTs), Transistor Biasing, Common Emitter Amplifier Analysis
*   **Week 14:** Field-Effect Transistors (FETs), MOSFET Characteristics, MOSFET Amplifier Analysis

**V. Digital Logic Design**

*   **Week 15:** Number Systems (Binary, Decimal, Hexadecimal), Logic Gates (AND, OR, NOT, NAND, NOR, XOR), Boolean Algebra
*   **Week 16:** Combinational Logic Circuit Design, Simplification Techniques (Karnaugh Maps), Adders, Multiplexers, Decoders
*   **Week 17:** Sequential Logic Circuit Design, Flip-Flops (SR, D, JK, T), Counters, Registers
*   **Week 18:** Introduction to Microcontrollers and Embedded Systems.
