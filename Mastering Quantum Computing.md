# Quantum Computing: Syllabus Outline

**I. Foundations & Mathematical Preliminaries**

*   Linear Algebra Review: Vectors, Matrices, Operators
*   Complex Numbers & Complex Vector Spaces
*   Probability & Statistics: Distributions, Random Variables
*   Information Theory Fundamentals: Bits, Entropy
*   Introduction to Computational Complexity: P, NP, BQP

**II. Quantum Mechanics Essentials**

*   Postulates of Quantum Mechanics
*   Quantum States, Superposition, Entanglement
*   Measurement in Quantum Mechanics
*   Time Evolution: Schrödinger Equation
*   Quantum Harmonic Oscillator (brief)

**III. Quantum Information & Computation**

*   Qubits & Quantum Registers
*   Quantum Gates: Single-Qubit & Multi-Qubit
*   Quantum Circuits & Quantum Algorithms
*   Quantum Teleportation & Superdense Coding
*   Quantum Key Distribution (QKD)

**IV. Quantum Algorithms**

*   Deutsch's Algorithm
*   Deutsch-Jozsa Algorithm
*   Grover's Search Algorithm: Theory & Implementation
*   Shor's Factoring Algorithm: Conceptual Overview
*   Quantum Fourier Transform (QFT)

**V. Quantum Computing Hardware & Technologies**

*   Superconducting Qubits: Transmons, Flux Qubits
*   Trapped Ions: Ion Traps, Laser Cooling
*   Photonic Qubits: Polarization, Entanglement Generation
*   Topological Qubits: Anyons, Braiding
*   Quantum Dot Qubits (brief)

**VI. Quantum Error Correction & Fault Tolerance**

*   Quantum Error Models: Bit-Flip, Phase-Flip
*   Quantum Error Correcting Codes: Shor Code, Steane Code
*   Surface Codes & Topological Error Correction
*   Fault-Tolerant Quantum Computation

**VII. Quantum Simulation**

*   Introduction to Quantum Simulation
*   Simulating Quantum Systems: Molecules, Materials
*   Adiabatic Quantum Computation & Quantum Annealing
*   Variational Quantum Eigensolver (VQE)

**VIII. Quantum Machine Learning**

*   Introduction to Quantum Machine Learning
*   Quantum Support Vector Machines (QSVM)
*   Quantum Neural Networks (QNN)
*   Quantum Principal Component Analysis (QPCA)

**IX. Advanced Topics & Research Directions**

*   Quantum Supremacy & Quantum Advantage
*   Near-Term Quantum Algorithms (NISQ)
*   Open Problems in Quantum Computing
*   Future Directions & Emerging Technologies
