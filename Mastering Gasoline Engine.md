# Gasoline Engine Syllabus

**I. Introduction to Internal Combustion Engines (ICEs)**
    *   Definition and Types of ICEs
    *   Gasoline Engine Overview: 4-Stroke Cycle
    *   Engine Components: Block, Head, Pistons, Valves, Crankshaft, Camshaft
    *   Engine Terminology: Bore, Stroke, Displacement, Compression Ratio

**II. The Four-Stroke Cycle**
    *   Intake Stroke: Valve Timing, Air-Fuel Mixture
    *   Compression Stroke: Increasing Pressure and Temperature
    *   Combustion Stroke (Power Stroke): Spark Ignition, Flame Propagation
    *   Exhaust Stroke: Valve Timing, Exhaust Gases

**III. Engine Components in Detail**
    *   Cylinder Head: Valves, Seats, Guides, Ports, Combustion Chamber Design
    *   Pistons, Rings, and Connecting Rods: Function, Materials, and Sealing
    *   Crankshaft and Bearings: Converting Linear to Rotational Motion
    *   Camshaft and Valve Train: Valve Actuation Mechanisms (OHV, OHC, DOHC)
    *   Engine Block: Materials, Coolant Passages, Oil Galleries

**IV. Fuel Systems**
    *   Carburetion: Principles, Components, Operation (Venturi effect, Jets, Float bowl)
    *   Fuel Injection:
        *   Types: Throttle Body Injection (TBI), Multi-Port Fuel Injection (MPFI), Direct Injection (GDI)
        *   Components: Fuel Pump, Fuel Filter, Fuel Injectors, Fuel Rail, Pressure Regulator
        *   Electronic Control: Engine Control Unit (ECU), Sensors, Actuators

**V. Ignition Systems**
    *   Purpose of Ignition System
    *   Components: Battery, Ignition Coil, Distributor (Traditional Systems), Spark Plugs
    *   Electronic Ignition Systems: Distributorless Ignition System (DIS), Coil-on-Plug (COP)
    *   Ignition Timing: Advance and Retard, Factors Affecting Timing
    *   Spark Plug Types and Operation

**VI. Lubrication System**
    *   Purpose of Lubrication
    *   Components: Oil Pan, Oil Pump, Oil Filter, Oil Galleries
    *   Types of Lubrication: Splash, Pressure
    *   Oil Viscosity and Grades: SAE Ratings
    *   Oil Additives and Function

**VII. Cooling System**
    *   Purpose of Cooling
    *   Types of Cooling Systems: Liquid Cooling, Air Cooling
    *   Components: Radiator, Water Pump, Thermostat, Cooling Fan, Coolant Passages
    *   Coolant Types and Properties
    *   Overheating Causes and Prevention

**VIII. Exhaust System**
    *   Purpose of Exhaust System
    *   Components: Exhaust Manifold, Catalytic Converter, Muffler, Tailpipe
    *   Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) System
    *   Emission Control Systems: Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV)

**IX. Engine Management Systems (EMS)**
    *   Sensors:
        *   Mass Airflow (MAF) Sensor
        *   Manifold Absolute Pressure (MAP) Sensor
        *   Throttle Position Sensor (TPS)
        *   Oxygen (O2) Sensor
        *   Crankshaft Position Sensor (CKP)
        *   Camshaft Position Sensor (CMP)
        *   Engine Coolant Temperature (ECT) Sensor
    *   Actuators: Fuel Injectors, Ignition Coils, Idle Air Control (IAC) Valve
    *   ECU Functions: Fuel Control, Ignition Control, Idle Speed Control, Emission Control
    *   On-Board Diagnostics (OBD): Diagnostic Trouble Codes (DTCs)

**X. Engine Performance and Diagnostics**
    *   Factors Affecting Engine Performance: Compression, Timing, Fuel Delivery
    *   Basic Diagnostic Procedures: Visual Inspection, Compression Test, Leak-Down Test
    *   Using Diagnostic Tools: Scan Tools, Multimeters, Oscilloscopes
    *   Troubleshooting Common Engine Problems

**XI. Engine Maintenance and Repair**
    *   Preventative Maintenance: Oil Changes, Tune-Ups
    *   Valve Adjustment
    *   Timing Belt/Chain Replacement
    *   Basic Engine Repairs: Gasket Replacement, Sensor Replacement

**XII. Alternative Fuels and Engine Technologies**
    *   Alternative Fuels: Ethanol, Biodiesel, CNG, LPG
    *   Hybrid Engine Technology
    *   Variable Valve Timing (VVT) and Lift (VVL) Systems
    *   Turbocharging and Supercharging
