# Deno Syllabus

**I. Introduction to Deno**

*   What is Deno? (Runtime, Goals, History)
*   Deno vs. Node.js (Key Differences & Similarities)

**IX. Deno Best Practices & Security Considerations**

**X. Real-World Deno Projects & Examples**
