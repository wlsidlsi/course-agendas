## Firearms Syllabus Outline

**Week 1: Introduction to Firearms**

*   History and Evolution of Firearms
*   Types of Firearms: Handguns, Rifles, Shotguns
*   Nomenclature: Parts of a Firearm (basic understanding)
*   Basic Safety Rules (Four Rules, etc.)

**Week 2: Ammunition**

*   Ammunition Components
*   Cartridge Types and Calibers (Overview)
*   Ammunition Selection Considerations

**Week 3: Handgun Fundamentals**

*   Grip and Stance
*   Sight Alignment and Sight Picture
*   Trigger Control
*   Breathing Techniques

**Week 4: Rifle Fundamentals**

*   Grip and Stance (Rifle Specific)
*   Sight Alignment and Sight Picture (Rifle Specific)
*   Trigger Control (Rifle Specific)
*   Breathing Techniques (Rifle Specific)

**Week 5: Shotgun Fundamentals**

*   Grip and Stance (Shotgun Specific)
*   Sight Picture (Shotgun Specific, Bead vs. Sights)
*   Trigger Control (Shotgun Specific)
*   Breathing Techniques (Shotgun Specific)

**Week 6: Safe Gun Handling**

*   Loading and Unloading Procedures (Various Firearms)
*   Clearing Malfunctions (Common Types)
*   Safe Storage and Transportation

**Week 7: Firearm Maintenance and Cleaning**

*   Basic Cleaning Procedures
*   Lubrication
*   Inspection for Damage

**Week 8: Legal Aspects of Firearm Ownership**

*   Federal Laws (Overview)
*   State and Local Laws (Overview – example jurisdiction)
*   Responsible Gun Ownership

**Week 9: Practical Application - Handgun Shooting**

*   Live Fire Exercises: Accuracy and Speed Drills
*   Malfunction Drills (Live Fire)
*   Stress Shooting (Simulated)

**Week 10: Practical Application - Rifle Shooting**

*   Live Fire Exercises: Accuracy and Speed Drills (Rifle)
*   Malfunction Drills (Live Fire, Rifle)
*   Distance Shooting (Rifle)

**Week 11: Practical Application - Shotgun Shooting**

*   Live Fire Exercises: Target Acquisition and Tracking (Shotgun)
*   Loading Drills (Shotgun)
*   Different Target Types (Shotgun: Clay, Paper)

**Week 12: Advanced Topics (Choose One or More)**

*   Concealed Carry Considerations
*   Home Defense Strategies
*   Competitive Shooting Overview (IPSC, IDPA, 3-Gun)

**Week 13: Scenario-Based Training**

*   Force-on-Force Scenarios (If available and appropriate)
*   Decision Making Under Pressure

**Week 14: Review and Examination**

*   Comprehensive Review of Course Material
*   Written Examination (Law, Safety, Knowledge)
*   Practical Examination (Safe Handling, Shooting Proficiency)
