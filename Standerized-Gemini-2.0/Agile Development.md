**II. Probability Rules and Theorems**

*   Mastering fundamental probability rules
    *   Addition rule for mutually exclusive and non-mutually exclusive events
        *   `P(A or B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A and B)`
    *   Multiplication rule for independent and dependent events
        *   `P(A and B) = P(A) * P(B|A)`
*   Applying conditional probability
    *   Understanding the concept of conditional probability
        *   `P(A|B) = P(A and B) / P(B)`
    *   Solving problems involving conditional probability
*   Bayes' Theorem
    *   Understanding Bayes' Theorem formula
        *   `P(A|B) = (P(B|A) * P(A)) / P(B)`
    *   Applying Bayes' Theorem to real-world problems, such as medical diagnosis.
