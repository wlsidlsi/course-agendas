**III. Analytic Geometry**

*   Conic Sections

    *   Hyperbolas: Standard form equation `(x^2/a^2) - (y^2/b^2) = 1`
    *   Identifying conic sections from their equations
*   Polar Coordinates
    *   Converting between rectangular and polar coordinates: `x = r*cos(theta)`, `y = r*sin(theta)`

    *   Equations of conic sections in polar form
*   Parametric Equations
    *   Understanding parametric equations: `x = f(t)`, `y = g(t)`
