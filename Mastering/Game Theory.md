**IV. Games with Incomplete Information**

*   Bayesian Games

    *   Designing games to achieve specific outcomes.

*   Evolutionary Game Theory
    *   Evolutionarily stable strategies (ESS).
    *   Replicator dynamics.

*   Behavioral Game Theory
