## **Module 1: Foundations: Divisibility and Primes**

**Week 1: Introduction to Number Theory**

*   What is Number Theory? Its scope and applications.
*   The set of integers. Basic operations and properties.
*   Mathematical induction: Principle of mathematical induction and its applications.
    *   *Practice:* Proofs by induction

**Week 2: Divisibility**

*   Divisibility and its properties: Definition, divisors, multiples.
*   The Division Algorithm: Quotient, remainder.
*   Greatest Common Divisor (GCD): Definition, methods to find GCD.
    *   *Practice:* Finding GCDs using different methods

**Week 3: Euclidean Algorithm**

*   The Euclidean Algorithm: A systematic method for finding GCD.
*   Bézout's Identity: Expressing GCD as a linear combination.
    *   *Practice:* Applying the Euclidean Algorithm and finding Bézout coefficients.

**Week 4: Prime Numbers**

*   Prime numbers: Definition, properties, and distribution.
*   The Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic: Unique factorization into primes.
    *   *Practice:* Prime factorization and applications.

## **Module 2: Congruences and Modular Arithmetic**

**Week 5: Introduction to Congruences**

*   Congruences: Definition, properties, and notation.
*   Residue Classes: Complete residue system, reduced residue system.
    *   *Practice:*  Solving basic congruences.

**Week 6: Linear Congruences**

*   Linear congruences: Solving linear congruences.
*   The Chinese Remainder Theorem (CRT): Solving systems of congruences.
    *   *Practice:*  Applying the Chinese Remainder Theorem.

**Week 7: Fermat's Little Theorem**

*   Fermat's Little Theorem: Statement and applications.
*   Euler's Theorem: Generalization of Fermat's Little Theorem.
    *   *Practice:* Using Fermat's Little Theorem and Euler's Theorem to simplify calculations.

**Week 8: Wilson's Theorem**

*   Wilson's Theorem: Statement and applications.
    *   *Practice:* Using Wilson's Theorem to simplify calculations.

## **Module 3: Arithmetic Functions and Diophantine Equations**

**Week 9: Arithmetic Functions**

*   Arithmetic functions: Definition and examples (e.g., φ(n), τ(n), σ(n)).
*   Multiplicative functions: Properties and examples.
    *   *Practice:* Calculating values of arithmetic functions.

**Week 10: The Mobius Inversion Formula**

*   The Mobius function: Definition and properties.
*   Mobius Inversion Formula: Statement and applications.
    *   *Practice:* Applying the Mobius Inversion Formula.

**Week 11: Diophantine Equations (Linear)**

*   Diophantine Equations: Introduction, types, and applications.
*   Linear Diophantine Equations: Solving linear Diophantine equations in two variables.
    *   *Practice:*  Finding solutions to linear Diophantine equations.

**Week 12: Diophantine Equations (Pythagorean Triples)**

*   Pythagorean Triples: Definition and generating formula.
    *   *Practice:*  Generating Pythagorean Triples.

## **Module 4: Advanced Topics (Choose at least one to dive deeper)**

*   Order of an integer modulo n.
*   Primitive roots: Definition and existence.

**Week 13: Quadratic Residues**

*   Quadratic residues and non-residues.
*   Legendre symbol: Definition and properties.
*   Quadratic reciprocity law (brief overview).

**Week 13: Continued Fractions**

*   Finite and infinite continued fractions.
*   Representing rational and irrational numbers.

**Week 14: Review and Advanced Problem Solving**
